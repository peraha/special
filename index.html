Crayfish recipes

River crayfish is a wonderful snack for beer, but this is not the only sphere of culinary application of these crustaceans. Recipes with crayfish are numerous and varied: soups and dishes on wok, salads and pastries, julienne and pasta. Meat is mainly contained in the neck (tail) of the cancer — this is about 1/5 of its total weight. There is a little meat in the pincers and quite a bit in the walking legs, although experts are happy to eat both the body of the cancer (the one under the shell) and its caviar.

INGREDIENTS
butter – 50 g
2 medium carrots
lemon juice
salt and white pepper to taste
100 ml of cream with a fat content of 33%
egg – 1 piece
5 egg yolks
sifted flour-2 cups
20 cancer necks
1 leek stalk (white part only)

Sift the flour with a slide on the Board, make a recess on top, pour in the egg and yolks. Knead the dough (you can add 1-2 tablespoons of water). Cover with cling film and let stand for 1-2 hours. Cut the leeks in half lengthwise and rinse. Cut the leeks and carrots into very small cubes, fry in a pan in butter over medium heat, 3-4 minutes. Pour in the cream, put the finely chopped cancer necks and simmer for another 5-6 minutes, until thick. Add lemon juice, salt and pepper. Unparliamentary. Roll out the dough very thinly or pass it through a paste machine. Cut out the mugs with a glass. Put 1 hour on one circle. L. minced meat, moisten the edges with water, cover with a second circle, carefully seal. Cook the ravioli in a large amount of salted boiling water for 2-3 minutes. Serve with butter. Tip: when the dough is rolled out, cover the layers with a damp towel or cling film – it dries very quickly.

INGREDIENTS

1 kg of tomatoes
100ml olive oil
1 tbsp red wine vinegar
12 cancer necks
1 cucumber
1 onion
8 black olives, pitted
2 sprigs of Basil
2 large garlic cloves
4 pieces of white bread
1 tbsp vegetable oil
salt, pepper

On tomatoes, make cross-shaped incisions, lower the fruit for 30 seconds. in boiling water. Then rinse with cold water and remove the skin. Wash and dry the Basil.
Mash the tomatoes and Basil in a blender. Continuing to whisk, add the olive oil, vinegar, pepper and salt. Pour into a bowl and place in refrigerator. Boil the cancer necks in boiling salted water for 4 minutes. Allow to cool. Cucumber and onion cut into small cubes, olives-thin circles.
Cut the garlic clove in half and RUB it on the bread. Fry the bread in hot oil until Golden brown.
Add the sliced cucumber, onion and olives to the soup and mix. Pour the soup on plates, put in the center of the cancer neck. Serve with hot garlic bread.
